# Brief — the spec

The authoritative statement of intent for this deck. Read this at the start of
any authoring session. Everything downstream (outline, slides) serves it.

## Audience

Who is in the room? What do they already know? What do they care about?

The audience is a local meetup I helped found in Burlington Vermont called ChatBTV. The purpose of the meetup is to build fellowship within the local software engineering community as AI transforms our industry. 

## Goal

What should the audience think, feel, or do after this talk? (One sentence.)

The audience should understand key insights on AI in software engineering from the 2026 DX Annual conference well enough to discuss them later in the meetup and research them further if desired.

## Core message

The 2026 DX Annual conference is a valuable resource of wisdom on where our industry is situated with regard to AI at this moment in history and how to make the most of that sitation.

## Visual Style

- dark background
- bold, colorful text
- graceful transitions
- sparse, tasteful animation
- generally reveal one line per click
  - except sub-lines, which should reveal together
  - and except where noted otherwise by a "Reveal" entry in the node

## Layout

- clean, uncluttered, highly readable
- less is more: favor fewer words
- move content to speaker notes if it gets wordy
- intent is that the verbal presentation is the main show - slides are a visual anchor for concepts presented verbally

## Constraints

Intent is a 20-minute talk followed by 40 minutes of discussion.
